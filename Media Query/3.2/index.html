Frankfurt University

Frankfurt University is a public research university located in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. It is one of the largest and most prestigious universities in the country, with over 45,000 students enrolled in over 100 undergraduate and graduate programs. Frankfurt University is known for its strong academic reputation and its commitment to research and innovation.
